About Me:

Hello there. My name is Madison Sherlock. I am a URI Student majoring in computer science and cybersecurity. Now, you may be wondering. Why did I want to pursue computer science? Well, let's start from the beginning. For almost all my life, I have played and enjoyed video games. Even when I was a baby, I was playing those Leapster edutainment games, so I've basically known them all my life. Anyway, all this video-game-playing made me want to make my own someday. However, I thought programming was too difficult for me to do, so I never went through with it. One day at highschool, I started to get back into the idea of wanting to learn how to code, not only because I wanted to make a game, but also because it sounded very fun. So, I enrolled in a C++ course highscool, and I really loved it. I loved it so much that I thought to myself "I wanna do this for a living." And so, I decided to major in computer science! Even though I'm probably not gonna make a video game anytime soon, I am programming, and I love it! I also really like to draw! I've been drawing almost all my life, but I've been taking drawing seriously for over 6 years...and counting! It's gotten to the point where I am prooud of every drawing I do!
